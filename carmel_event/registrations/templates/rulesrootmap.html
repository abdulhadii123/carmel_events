📜 Race Rules & Guidelines

💡 Follow these guidelines for a smooth and enjoyable race experience!

📍 Check-in Timings & Procedure

⏰ Arrival Time: Runners must check in at least 45 minutes before their race category starts.

🎽 Bib Collection: Ensure your Bib is securely attached to your T-shirt.

🏃 Race Etiquette

  • ✔️ Follow the designated race route.
  • ✔️ Respect fellow runners and event volunteers.
  • ✔️ Littering is strictly prohibited—use waste bins along the route.

🚫 Disqualification Criteria

  • ❌ Running in the wrong category.
  • ❌ Using unauthorized vehicles/bicycles on the route.
  • ❌ Not wearing the official race bib.

📌 These rules ensure fair competition & a great experience for all!
